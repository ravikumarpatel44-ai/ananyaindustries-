About us

Lead, kept in circulation.

Founded in 2022, Aanya Industries is an Ahmedabad-based lead recycler. We collect used lead-acid batteries and industrial scrap, then dismantle, smelt and refine them into manufacturer-grade lead — all under one roof.

Our work is simple: keep lead in circulation and out of the ground. Every tonne we refine replaces a tonne that would otherwise be mined — better for our partners, and better for the planet.

How it works

From scrap to refined lead.

A controlled, three-stage loop that keeps recovery high and emissions within norms.

01

Collect

We arrange pick-up of your batteries and scrap, weigh on arrival, and document every consignment.

02

Dismantle

Batteries are broken and separated — acid neutralised, plastics recovered, lead components sorted.

03

Smelt

Lead is reduced in rotary furnaces fitted with scrubbers that keep emissions within CPCB norms.
