Our story

A recycler with roots.

Founded in 2022 on a simple conviction — the lead the world already has is far too valuable to bury — Aanya Industries is built to keep it in circulation.

At our Ahmedabad plant, spent lead-acid batteries and industrial scrap are collected, dismantled, smelted and refined into manufacturer-grade lead, all under one roof.

We supply battery makers, foundries and bulk consumers across India — returning recovered lead to the products people rely on every day.

What guides us

Mission, vision & values.

The principles behind every tonne we recover.

◆Our mission

To return as much lead to industry as possible — recovered cleanly, refined to spec, and delivered without compromise on safety.

◆Our vision

A circular lead economy where mining is the exception, not the rule, and recycled metal is the default for manufacturers.

◆Our values

Compliance first, fair dealing always, and durable engineering over shortcuts. We'd rather do it properly than do it twice.
